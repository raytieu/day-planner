Work Day Scheduler

A simple calendar app for scheduling your work day.


Timeblocks are color-coded, by hour, as follows: Past Current Future

Press the Save button at the end of each timeblock to save that hour's detail.


Click the "Clear Timeblocks" button to clear your work-day details.

Click the "Refresh Timeblocks" button to reload the page with your saved work-day details.

Click the "Save All Timeblocks" to save all timeblocks with one click.
